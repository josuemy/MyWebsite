Some of my projects

Stage Presence

How many times have you needed to give an important speech? Now, how many courses have you taken on the art of giving presentations? If the answer is "none", or "not many", there's nothing to worry about. We've developed Stage Presence - a web platform that helps you practice a presentation of your choosing until it's perfect.

Stage Presence was this year's UCSB Capstone winner! I was in charge of the app's Video module which tracked the user's movements and facial sentiment. I was also responsible for the frontend design and overall look of the app. If you are interested in knowing more about this or any other project, make sure to click on the project's title :)

Corepool

During my internship at Procore, I had the opportunity to develop a mobile app that improved the parking and commuting logistics for the company's employees. I worked along with other 3 interns who shared my passion to learn new technologies and develop a good quality product. By the end of a week and a half, our product was completely functional and ready to be used.

I designed and implemented an intuitive UI based on user experience research. Additionally, I also implemented JavaScript promises to avoid wrong data delivery caused by asynchronous calls to the database.

Lobster

Lobster is a chat app designed for you and your significant other. With Lobster, you can create a virtual room exclusively for you two where you'll be able to share daily thoughts, pictures, and anything you want without the distraction of other people.

I designed the firebase database schema for users and chat rooms. I like apps where you can have a user's profile so I used Glide and Firebase's Cloud Storage to let users have an avatar and share images.

Cowbell

Cowbell is an alarm clock app for Android. Cowbell helps you wake up while it helps you learn with its three different wake up options. You'll be able to create and customize a set of fill-in the blank, or multiple choice cards that will pop up at the time the alarm goes off. Answer correctly in order to shut down the alarm!

Chronooz

Don't know what the latest news are talking about? Are you missing context to understand them? Chronooz gathers popular news from more than 58 sources and gives you a timeline of related events. Everything to catch up with current events is now in one place! I learned Ruby on Rails from scratch over the course of 24 hours and built this simple webapp with a friend of mine.
