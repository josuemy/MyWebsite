Some of my projects

Corepool

During my internship at Procore, I had the opportunity to develop a mobile app that improved the parking and commuting logistics for the company's employees. I worked along with other 3 interns who shared my passion to learn new technologies and develop a good quality product. By the end of a week and a half, our product was completely functional and ready to be used.

Cowbell

Cowbell is an alarm clock app for Android. Cowbell helps you wake up while it helps you learn with its three different wake up options. The user can create and customize a set of fill-in the blank or multiple choice cards that will pop up at the time the alarm goes off. The user will have to answer correctly in order to shut down the alarm!

Chronooz

Don't know what the latest news are talking about? Are you missing context to understand them? Chronooz gathers popular news from more than 58 sources and gives you a timeline of related events. Everything to catch up with current events is now in one place! I learned Ruby on Rails from scratch over the course of 24 hours and build this simple webapp with a friend of mine.
